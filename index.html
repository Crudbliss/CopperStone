The Process

How MATRIX Works

The Framework

Elmore's Four Modes of Learning

MATRIX is built on Richard Elmore's framework from Harvard GSE, which categorizes learning along two dimensions: the authority structure (Hierarchical vs. Distributed) and the social structure (Individual vs. Collective).

📚 Hierarchical Individual

Structured lessons • Direct instruction

💻 Distributed Individual

Self-paced • Tech-enabled

🤝 Hierarchical Collective

Group learning • Teacher-guided

🧠 Distributed Collective

Peer-driven • Inquiry-based
